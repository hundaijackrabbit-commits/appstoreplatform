## Implementation Strategy: What Your Plumbing Website Needs

### Essential Pages for Plumbing Websites

**1. Home Page**
- Clear statement of services and service area
- Emergency contact number prominently displayed
- Customer testimonials or reviews
- Years in business and licensing information
- Call-to-action for estimates or emergency service

**2. Services Page**
- **Emergency services**: drain cleaning, leak repair, pipe bursts
- **Repair services**: faucets, toilets, water heaters, garbage disposals
- **Installation services**: new fixtures, appliances, pipe replacement
- **Maintenance services**: inspections, preventive care, system optimization

**3. Service Areas Page**
- List all cities and neighborhoods you serve
- Response time estimates for each area
- Service radius clearly defined
- Local landmarks and major areas mentioned

**4. About Us Page**
- Owner background and experience
- Business history and community involvement
- Team member photos and qualifications
- Licensing, bonding, and insurance information

**5. Gallery/Portfolio Page**
- Before/after photos of completed work
- Different types of projects (emergency, installation, repair)
- Quality workmanship demonstrations
- Customer testimonials with photos

**6. Contact Page**
- Multiple ways to contact (phone, email, contact form)
- Business address and service areas
- Hours of operation (including emergency availability)
- Response time commitments
