## 🎯 Brand System Specifications

### **Color System**
- **Primary Green**: #00D562 (Brand accent, CTAs)
- **Primary Dark**: #0A0A0A (Backgrounds)  
- **Primary White**: #FFFFFF (Text, clean areas)
- **Accent Blue**: #3B82F6 (Trust elements)

### **Typography Hierarchy**
- **Headlines**: Arial Bold, 48px (H1), 32px (H2)
- **Body Text**: Arial Regular, 18px
- **CTAs**: Arial Medium, 16px

### **Layout Principles**
- 8px grid system for consistent spacing

- Center-aligned, modern SaaS aesthetic
- High contrast for accessibility
